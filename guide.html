User Guide

Features:

1. At the top is the navigation bar. The page that is highlighted is the page you are on. To access another page, click on the one you would like to go to.

2. To look through a page, scroll up and down to see the content.

3. To use the music player on the playlist page, press the play button to play the song. Press it again to pause/stop the song. To access the next song, press the forward button. To access the previous song, press the prevoius button. You can adjust the volume with the volume button.

4. To use the message box, click on the box and type out a message. Then, press send.

Content:

Home Page: This page is about me. There is a blurb that introduces me and there is information about various things that I like.

Toronto Page: This page shows the special spots in Toronto that I enjoy going to. Along with the recommendations, there are pictures and information about the places.

Art Page: This page is about my artwork. It includes pictures of different styles of art that I've done over the years.

Playlist Page: This page has a music player that includes 5 songs that I enjoy listening. It is user-friendly and interactive, allowing you to listen to the songs.

Contact Page: This page allows you to send a message to me.
