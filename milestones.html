Toronto Music Garden: The Toronto Music Garden is often an overlooked park when people go visit harbourfront. It's a calm and peaceful place to read, take a stroll, and relax. A nice place to see the sunset and listen to music at the end of the park awaits you.

Distillery District: Although it's cold, the distillery district is the most beautiful during the Winter. There's many holiday treats to try and a big Christmas tree surrounded by fairy lights.

Thomas Fisher Rare Book Library: A beautiful library at the University of Toronto that houses many rare books and special manuscripts that are worth exploring.

The Monkey's Paw: The Monkey's Paw is an antique book store specializing in uncommon books and paper artifacts. Inside you can find the BiblioMat, the world's first randomizing vending machine for old books. Perfect for old books lovers.
