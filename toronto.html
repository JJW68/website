Life in Toronto

Favorite things about Toronto

Despite the lack of tourist attractions in Toronto, I still love exploring downtown. Toronto is one of the most diverse cities in the world and there's so much to experience. Other than the famous CN Tower, there are various hidden corners and spots waiting to be discovered; each with its own unique aspect to it.

Although it's cold, the distillery district is the most beautiful during the Winter. There's many holiday treats to try and a big Christmas tree surrounded by fairy lights.

A beautiful library at the University of Toronto that houses many rare books and special manuscripts that are worth exploring.

The Monkey's Paw is an antique book store specializing in uncommon books and paper artifacts. Inside you can find the BiblioMat, the world's first randomizing vending machine for old books. Perfect for old books lovers.
